<div class="info-section">
  <h2>About Our Research Group</h2>
  <p>
    The Intelligent Quantum Cloud Computing Laboratory (iQCloudLab) is led by Dr. Rahul Yadav at Harbin Engineering University. Our lab focuses on cutting-edge research in Quantum Cloud Computing, Agentic Edge Intelligence, Distributed Systems, and Digital Twin technologies. We offer a collaborative, international environment where students engage in high-impact research with opportunities for publications in premier venues and industry partnerships.
  </p>
  
  <h3>Research Areas</h3>
  <p>Prospective students can expect to work on projects in:</p>
  <ul style="color: #475569; font-size: 1.05rem; line-height: 2; padding-left: 25px;">
    <li><strong>Quantum Cloud Computing:</strong> Hybrid quantum-classical algorithms, quantum resource orchestration, QoS-aware scheduling</li>
    <li><strong>Agentic Edge Computing:</strong> LLM-based autonomous agents, intelligent task offloading, multi-agent coordination</li>
    <li><strong>Distributed Computing:</strong> Fog-cloud continuum, energy-efficient architectures, distributed resource management</li>
    <li><strong>Digital Twins:</strong> IoT-enabled frameworks, real-time synchronization, predictive maintenance systems</li>
  </ul>
</div>
